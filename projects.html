From the time I project-ed my ideas:

Gesture Enabled Smart Glove

This project showcases meticulous work in establishing connections, thorough testing, and effective communication of flex sensors and their respective angles. By focusing on these aspects, the glove can accurately detect and interpret different sign language gestures based on the degree of finger bending. Our dedication to fine-tuning the sensor angles ensures precise recognition of gestures, enhancing the usability and reliability of the device. Being selected to showcase our project to an esteemed panel from the USA is a testament to the innovation and quality of our work, highlighting its potential impact and significance in the field of assistive technology.

Medical Drone Hub Network

Introducing the Medical Drone Hub Network, a project ideated by myself, recently awarded the prestigious 3rd position at the Ideathon. This innovative initiative revolutionizes healthcare delivery by leveraging hybrid drones equipped with state-of-the-art technology to establish a comprehensive network of drone hubs in remote and underserved areas. By seamlessly integrating telemedicine facilities, emergency response capabilities, and real-time communication systems, this project aims to bridge the gap in access to healthcare services, particularly in regions with limited infrastructure or geographical barriers. Through strategic partnerships and innovative solutions, we aim to transform the healthcare outcomes of remote areas.

Yoga Pose Detection Model

Designed and implemented a Real-Time Pose Detection and Classification System, utilizing advanced OpenCV and MediaPipe algorithms to accurately identify and interpret human poses. This innovative solution features a responsive user interface and a structured practice timer, enhancing user engagement and training efficiency. The project aims to provide a robust and precise tool for real-time visual feedback and performance analytics across various platforms.
